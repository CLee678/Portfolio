About Me

Hello! My legal name is Wing Yee Lee, but I like to go by Cayley.

I was born and raised in Hong Kong. In 2010, I came to the U.S. to pursue my bachelor’s degree. The knowledge and skills I acquired during my undergraduate studies provided me with the foundation to start my career in GIS. I worked for the Hong Kong government as a Cartographic Technical Officer, where I collaborated with colleagues on various impactful projects. These included managing slope maintenance responsibility information, assisting with new railway projects, participating in artificial island projects, and identifying archaeological sites. My six years of experience solidified my understanding of how GIS can be utilized to address real-world challenges.

I believe in the necessity of continuous learning to stay competitive and relevant, especially as the GIS field is constantly expanding and evolving. Therefore, I decided to attend graduate school to deepen my knowledge in geospatial data science, strengthen my GIS skills, and expand my career prospects. My goal is to obtain not only a Master’s degree but also a GIS Professional (GISP) Certification from the GIS Certification Institute (GISCI). Achieving this certification will not only validate my skills but also enhance my professional credibility in the GIS community. I am committed to lifelong learning and professional development to ensure I remain at the forefront of the GIS industry.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. If you are interested in discussing potential job opportunities or collaborations, please feel free to contact me at tur91128@temple.edu. I am enthusiastic about the opportunity to contribute to the company and believe that my skills are well-aligned with the team. I look forward to connecting with you!
